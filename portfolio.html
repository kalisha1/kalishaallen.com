Portfolio Item BBC Crafthouse Campaign

Project Description

BBC is known for creating unique dining atmospheres specific to the neighborhoods of each of their locations. This concept will be echoed in the opening of their fourth location in June of 2014, on trendy Frankfort Avenue. The new BBC Craft House will partner with other local breweries and boast an impressive forty craft brew taps. Their main partner will be Country Boy, a brewery out of Lexington, Kentucky. They will also feature the New Albanian Brewing Company and Great Flood Brewing, which is set to open its doors in Louisville this spring.
