BBC CRAFT HOUSE DESIGNS

Project Description

For my senior class project I took on the role of creative director, where my focus was on creative spec, such as designing a logo, social media content and print advertisements. My client was BBC, a local brewpub in Louisville, KY. Our client had plans to open up a new brewpub, BBC Craft House that has 40 local craft beers on tap. Below you will find a sample of the creative spec, I created using Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Canva and Sketch.

Key Messages

  • BBC Crafthouse is the place to talk about beer
  • Local craft beer partnerships
  • Brand New Location
  • Over 40 craft beers on tap
